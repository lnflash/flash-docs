## Flash Mobile App Training

This page is a quick overview of the current Flash app for new users.

## What users can do in the app

- Create and upgrade an account
- Use the **Cash Wallet** for USD balances
- Use the **Bitcoin Wallet** for BTC send and receive
- Swap between Cash Wallet and Bitcoin Wallet
- Top up from a bank transfer or Flashpoint
- Cash out to a bank account
- Chat with other Flash users
- Find merchants and Flashpoints on the map
- Use a Flashcard at supported merchants

## Recommended onboarding order

1. **Get Started** — download the app and sign in
2. **Upgrade Your Account** — unlock Pro or Merchant features
3. **Cash Wallet** — understand your USD balance
4. **Bitcoin Wallet** — send and receive Bitcoin
5. **Swap** — move value between wallets when needed
6. **Top Up / Cash Out** — move between your bank and Flash

## Important note

The Cash Wallet and Bitcoin Wallet are different. If you are not sure which one to use, start with the wallet guides:
- [Cash Wallet](../guides/cash-wallet)
- [Bitcoin Wallet](../guides/bitcoin-wallet)
- [Swap Between Wallets](../guides/swap)
