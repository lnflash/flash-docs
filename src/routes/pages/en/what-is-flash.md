## What is Flash?

Flash is a Bitcoin service provider building financial products specifically for the Caribbean. Our flagship product is the **Flash app** — a mobile wallet that lets you send, receive, and manage Bitcoin using the Lightning Network.

Flash is built for everyday people. You don't need a bank account to get started. You don't need to understand Bitcoin deeply. You just need your phone.

---

## Account Levels

Flash has four account levels. You start at Trial and can upgrade at any time.

| Level | Name | What you can do |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| L0 | Trial | Send and receive Bitcoin, explore the app |
| L1 | Personal | Phone-verified, higher limits |
| L2 | Pro | Top up from bank, cash out to bank |
| L3 | Merchant | All Pro features + merchant tools, accept payments |

To upgrade, see [How to Upgrade Your Account](guides/upgrade-account).

---

## The Flash Ecosystem

**Flash App** — The main mobile wallet for individuals. Available on iOS and Android.

**Flashpoint** — Merchants in the Flash network. You can top up or cash out in person at any Flashpoint location.

**Flashcard** — A rewards card linked to your Flash wallet. Use it at Flashpoint merchants to earn and spend rewards.

**Flash POS** — Point-of-sale app for merchants to accept Flash payments.

---

## Who is Flash for?

- **Individuals** who want a simple way to hold and use Bitcoin
- **Caribbean residents** who want to send or receive money without high fees
- **Merchants** who want to accept payments without expensive hardware or monthly fees
- **Anyone** who wants to move money faster and cheaper than traditional banking

---

## Where is Flash available?

Flash is currently focused on **Jamaica**, with expansion across the Caribbean planned. The app is available globally on iOS and Android.
