## Sending Bitcoin

Flash makes it easy to send Bitcoin to anyone, anywhere. There are three ways to send:

---

## 1. Send to a Flash Username

The simplest way to send to another Flash user.

1. Tap **Send** in the Flash app
2. Type the recipient's Flash username
3. Enter the amount
4. Review and tap **Confirm**

The payment is instant and free between Flash users.

---

## 2. Send via Lightning Invoice

To pay a Lightning invoice from any wallet:

1. Tap **Send**
2. Paste the Lightning invoice or scan the QR code
3. Review the amount and fee
4. Tap **Confirm**

Lightning payments settle in seconds with minimal fees.

---

## 3. Send On-Chain Bitcoin

To send to a standard Bitcoin address:

1. Tap **Send**
2. Paste the Bitcoin address or scan the QR code
3. Enter the amount
4. Review the network fee
5. Tap **Confirm**

On-chain transactions may take 10 to 60 minutes to confirm depending on network conditions.

---

## Tips

- Double-check the recipient before confirming — Bitcoin transactions cannot be reversed
- Lightning is faster and cheaper for everyday payments
- On-chain is better for larger amounts or when the recipient does not have Lightning

For help receiving Bitcoin, see [Your Bitcoin Wallet](guides/bitcoin-wallet).
