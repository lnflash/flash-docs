## Lightning Wallet Technology

Flash uses modern Lightning wallet technology to let you send and receive Bitcoin quickly.

For most users, the important part is simple:

- open your **Bitcoin Wallet**
- send or receive Bitcoin
- keep an eye on your transactions

## What the app does for you

Flash handles the complicated parts for you:

- payment routing
- wallet state
- transaction tracking
- recovery and deposit handling

## What users do not need to worry about

You do not need to manage channels or routing manually.

You do not need to know the technical details to use Flash.
