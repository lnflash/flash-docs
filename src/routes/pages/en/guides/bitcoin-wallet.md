## What is the Bitcoin Wallet?

Flash has two separate wallets: a **Cash Wallet** (for JMD/USD) and a **Bitcoin Wallet**. This guide covers the Bitcoin Wallet.

The Bitcoin Wallet is powered by the Lightning Network and lets you hold, send, and receive Bitcoin directly — without conversion to dollars.

> **Note:** The Bitcoin Wallet must be enabled before you can use it. See below.

---

## Enable the Bitcoin Wallet

1. Open the Flash app and go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Advanced**
3. Enable the **Bitcoin Wallet** toggle
4. The Bitcoin Wallet will now appear in your app alongside your Cash Wallet

---

## Receiving Bitcoin

### Lightning (Recommended)

1. Open the Flash app and tap **Receive**
2. Select the **Bitcoin Wallet**
3. Your Lightning invoice and QR code will appear
4. Share the QR code or copy the invoice to the sender
5. Bitcoin arrives in your wallet instantly once paid

### On-Chain Bitcoin

For senders using a standard Bitcoin wallet (not Lightning):

1. Tap **Receive** and select **Bitcoin Wallet**
2. Tap the receive type selector and choose **On-Chain**
3. Share your Bitcoin address or QR code
4. Funds arrive after network confirmations — typically 10–60 minutes

> 💡 **Tip:** Lightning is faster and cheaper for most payments. Use on-chain only when the sender requires it.

---

## Sending Bitcoin

1. Tap **Send** in the Flash app
2. Select the **Bitcoin Wallet** as your source
3. Paste a Lightning invoice, scan a QR code, or enter a Flash username
4. Review the amount and fee
5. Tap **Confirm** to send

Lightning payments are instant. On-chain sends may take 10–60 minutes to confirm.

## Unclaimed Deposits

Sometimes an on-chain deposit cannot be claimed automatically — for example, if network fees spike above the deposit amount. These appear in the app as **Unclaimed Deposits**.

### Deposit status meanings

- 🟡 **Auto-claiming** — the app is processing it automatically, no action needed
- 🟠 **Approval required** — fee is higher than expected, you need to approve it
- 🔴 **Claim failed** — needs manual action

### Approval Required

1. Tap **Details** on the deposit
2. Review the fee breakdown — deposit amount, network fee, and what you'll receive
3. Tap **Approve** to claim, or **Refund** to send funds to an external wallet

### Claim Failed

1. Tap **Details**
2. Tap **Refund** to recover funds to an external Bitcoin wallet

## Bitcoin Wallet vs Cash Wallet

| | Bitcoin Wallet | Cash Wallet |
|--|---------------|-------------|
| Currency | Bitcoin (sats) | JMD / USD |
| Top Up | Lightning or on-chain BTC | Bank transfer, Flashpoint |
| Cash Out | Send to any BTC/Lightning wallet | Bank transfer (Pro/Merchant) |
| Best for | Holding Bitcoin, paying Bitcoin | Everyday spending, bank transfers |

See [Cash Wallet](cash-wallet) for the Cash Wallet guide.

## Troubleshooting

**"Wallet is offline"**
Check your internet connection and reopen the app. Your balance is safe.

**My Lightning payment hasn't arrived**
Lightning payments are usually instant. If it has been more than a few minutes, contact support with the invoice details.
