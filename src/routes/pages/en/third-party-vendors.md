## Flash Partners

Flash has worked with a number of technology partners over time. Some of those integrations are now legacy, and some remain part of the current stack.

### Current or recent building blocks

- **Breez SDK** — helps power the Lightning wallet experience
- **IBEX Mercado** — helps power the USD cash wallet and payment infrastructure

### Legacy / historical integrations

- **Galoy Project**
- **BTCPay Server**
