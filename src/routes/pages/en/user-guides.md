Welcome to the Flash user guides. Everything you need to get started and make the most of your Flash account.

## Getting Set Up

- [Upgrade Your Account](guides/upgrade-account) — Unlock cashout, top-up, and merchant features by upgrading to Pro or Merchant
- [Top Up](guides/top-up) — Add funds to your Flash wallet via bank transfer or at a Flashpoint
- [Cash Out](guides/cash-out) — Send your Flash balance to your bank account

## Using Your Bitcoin Wallet

- [Bitcoin Wallet](guides/bitcoin-wallet) — Send and receive Bitcoin via Lightning or on-chain
- [Non-Custodial Wallets](guides/non-custodial-wallets) — Move funds to self-custody for larger amounts
- [Dollar Cost Averaging](guides/dca) — Automatically buy Bitcoin on a schedule

## App Features

- [Flash Chat](guides/chat) — Encrypted messaging with built-in Bitcoin payments
- [Find Locations](guides/map) — Find Flash merchants and exchange points near you
- [Flashcard](guides/flashcard) — Use your Flash rewards card for purchases
- [Earn Bitcoin](guides/earn) — Ways to earn Bitcoin through Flash
